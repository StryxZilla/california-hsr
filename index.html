The Bottom Line

What Voters Were Promised (2008)

  • SF to LA in 2 hours 40 minutes
  • $33 billion total cost
  • Complete by 2020
  • No operating subsidies needed

Reality (2026)

  • Zero passengers transported
  • $89-128 billion projected cost
  • Maybe Merced-Bakersfield by 2033
  • SF-LA date: "TBD"

The Funding Gap: Even for just the Central Valley segment (Merced-Bakersfield), the project faces a potential $10.2 billion budget gap according to recent reports.
